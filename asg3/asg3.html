Note:
This is Charlie a baby sheep.
Click the canvas to lock the mouse (look around); press Escape to unlock.
WASD or Arrow Keys to move. Space = up, Shift = down.
Q/E to pan. When mouse is locked, move mouse to look (yaw and pitch).
R to add block, F to remove block (at current cell or move crosshair to block).
Skybox and Floor are textured.
